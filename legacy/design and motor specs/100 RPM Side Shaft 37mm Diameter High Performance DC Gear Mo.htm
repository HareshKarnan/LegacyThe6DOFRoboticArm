Introduction

100 RPM Side Shaft 37mm Diameter High Performance DC Gear Motor is suitable for small robots / automation systems. It has sturdy construction with gear box built to handle stall torque produced by the motor. Drive shaft is supported from both sides with metal bushes. Motor runs smoothly from 4V to 12V and gives 100 RPM at 12V. Motor has 6mm diameter, 18mm length drive shaft with D shape for excellent coupling.

Important Note: This motor will be bit noisy while running. For long life, this motor is not recommended for application requiring dynamic torque of more than 6kg-cm.

Specifications

  • RPM: 100 at 12V
  • Voltage: 4V to 12V
  • Stall torque: 36Kg-cm at maximum limited stall current of 4Amp.
  • Shaft diameter: 6mm
  • Shaft length: 18mm
  • Gear assembly: Spur
  • Brush type: Carbon
